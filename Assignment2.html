The Last Prophet Muhammad ❤❤❤ (Peace Be upon him) with his Companions

Muhammad (Peace Be upon him) is the final prophet in Islam, known as the 'Seal of the Prophets'. This means that Muslims regard Muhammad as Allah's final messenger. The Qur'an is formed from the revelations Muhammad received from Allah through the Angel Jibril.

There were 4 companions who selected for the KHILAFAT after one another.It is also called KHILAFAT E RASHIDA. There names mentioned below according to the period of khilafat .

  1. 1st  KHALIFA Hazrat ABU BAKRرضی اللہ تعالی عنہ
  2. 2nd  KHALIFA Hazrat Umer Farooqرضی اللہ تعالی عنہ
  3. 3rd  KHALIFA Hazrat Usman E Ghaniرضی اللہ تعالی عنہ
  4. 4th  KHALIFA Hazrat ALI رضی اللہ تعالی عنہ

    Period Of KHILAFAT

  • Hazrat ABU BAKR   2 years and 3 months and 9 days.
  • Hazrat Umer Farooq   10 years 5 months.
  • Hazrat Usman E Ghani   12 Years.
  • Hazrat ALI    5 years and 10 months.
